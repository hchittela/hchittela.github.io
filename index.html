The story.

Hey! I'm Hemanth, a sophomore studying computer science and cognitive science at UPenn. As a student I get to pursue my interests and learn from those around me – exactly what I love doing.

My story started out with the “played with Legos since I was a kid" situation. Over the years however, the piles of bolts and raw materials in my room receded to give way to electronics and books on coding. I'm now captivated by software and computer science.  I've found a bit of a niche for myself, studying both CS  and cognitive science to focus on their interaction and application to artificial intelligence.

From a technical standpoint, I’m adept at object-oriented programming (Java and C#), comfortable with functional programming (OCaml), and have used the ASP.NET MVC framework in conjunction with SQL databases. It's all detailed in my resume (over to the right).

Aside from a rigorous course load, on campus I'm involved with a fusion dance team, TEDxPenn, and am part of an aerial robitics club.

I love a good challenge and strive to keep things interesting.
